Overview

Purpose

This elevator pitch will be for a music page for myself. I am a metal musician and this will be a website that introduces who I am and my music/messages. The webpage needs to be inviting, clean, neat and informational. The main home page will contain a picture of me and a summary of who I am. There will also be links in the header that say “home”, “music”, and “videos”. Each page will have some social media links at the bottom. After giving my presentation to three individuals, they suggested to me to give some more description of how the website is going to look but besides that they said that my pitch was good.

Audience

Those who might enjoy my music.

Content

Home page

Header: The header will contain my logo and the navigation bar that will contain links to other pages. The links that will be in the header will be “Home”, “Music, and “About Me”. The purpose of this header is to make different pages and information easily seen so that people know where to go to find out more about me or my music. (The header will be the same for all pages)

Content: Below the header, there will be a vertical photo of me in the center and on the sides there will be pictures that will have borders and box shadows. These pictures will be the covers of the most recent music that I have released and there will be links below each one that link to YouTube, Itunes, and Spotify. People that come to my website will be able to quickly know where to go to listen to my music. Below this there will be some text where I share a good scripture.There is not going to be much text on this page and the quote will be the only text on the page that won’t be a link.

Ephesians 5:8 For at one time you were darkness, but now you are light in the Lord. Walk as children of light.

Footer: The footer will contain some links to the other pages and there will also be some social media links as well as some more links to YouTube, Spotify, and Itunes. (The footer will be the same for all pages)

About Me

Content: On this page, there will be some basic information about where I am from and then there will also be some information about my musical history. I’ll explain when and how I got into music and when I started to learn different instruments and how I formed a band at one point in time. This will all lead up until my officially released songs and to the present day. If people listen to my music and are curious about my history, they will have an easy method of learning more about me.

Origins: I was born in August of 2001 in Western Washington and I spent my first 20 years in Washington. Even from a young age, I had a passion for music. I was always either dancing to music or spending time listening to music on the computer or on the old school ipods. When I was 7 years old, my mother started to teach me how to play the piano and I eventually started to take piano lessons. Like many other kids, I did not enjoy the music that I was playing and after 2 years, I quit. Eventually I decided to pursue running as a passion like much of my family had but when I was 14, I ran into some medical problems that caused me extreme fatigue and I wasn’t able to run like I wanted to. I turned to music to give me a purpose.

Music History: Before this time in my life, I had enjoyed a variety of music but it wasn’t until this time of my life that I started to get emotionally invested in rock music. I started to listen to bands like Linkin Park and Fall Out Boy religiously and this music helped me get through some dark times. I eventually started to take guitar lessons and I eventually started to have a passion for metal. I started to listen to bands like A Day To Remember and Of Mice and Men. I heard the unclean vocals or screams in their songs and I wanted to learn how to do that so I decided to teach myself how to scream.

At 16 years old, I took a piano class at my high school and I gained a new love for piano that I did not have before and I also started to learn how to play bass guitar.

While I did enjoy learning songs, I was obsessed with writing my own songs and I even created a band named “Evergreen” in 2017. We only played one show at our High School and we didn't release any songs. Our band didn’t last long and I realized that if I wanted to write songs, I was going to do everything myself.

Released Music: In October 2019, I started to write my own EP. I finished the EP in August of 2020 and I released it on Soundcloud but after a couple of weeks, I decided that it wasn’t ready yet. I deleted the EP and I started to write new songs. I wrote a new EP named Generic that was released January of 2021. From July to December of 2021, I released 4 singles.

What Now?: I am currently going to school at Brigham Young University to pursue a Computer Science degree. Definitely don’t have as much time for music that I used to but I am continually trying to write more songs.
